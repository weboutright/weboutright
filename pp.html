We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our website contact form or email us directly. We will respond to your inquiry within a reasonable timeframe.
